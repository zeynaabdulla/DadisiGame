Find the words to help Maya!

"Integers" are the whole numbers that you see and count everyday, like 1, 2, 3, and 4!
"String" is another word for... words! They represent letters, words, and mixtures of different numbers and letters.
"Apple", "Video game", and "ABC123" are all strings!
"Booleans" represent things that are true or false.
"Doubles" are another word for decimal or fraction numbers, like 1.2, 2.5, and 1/2.
"Code" is what programmers like Maya write to create cool things like phone apps, websites, and even games!
